SOURDOUGH BREAD RECIPE NOTES

Ingredients:
- flour (bread flour or all-purpose)
- water
- salt
- active sourdough starter

Method summary:
Feed the starter, then mix flour and water for an autolyse. Add starter and salt,
knead until gluten develops, and bulk ferment until the dough rises and shows
bubbles. Perform stretch-and-folds every 30 minutes. Shape the loaf, proof in a
basket, and bake in a preheated dutch oven.

Baking details: 475°F covered for 20 minutes, then uncovered for 20–25 minutes.
Good sourdough has a crisp crust, open crumb, and tangy flavor.
