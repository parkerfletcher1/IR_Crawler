PERSONAL BUDGETING NOTES

A simple budget starts with income, fixed expenses, and variable expenses.
Fixed costs include rent, insurance, and subscriptions. Variable costs include
groceries, fuel, dining, and entertainment.

Track spending weekly. Build an emergency fund (3–6 months of expenses) and pay
down high-interest debt first. Consider saving goals: retirement contributions,
a down payment, or a vacation fund. Automate transfers so saving happens before
spending.

Keywords: cash flow, savings rate, interest, debt payoff, investment portfolio,
index funds, risk tolerance, and diversification.
