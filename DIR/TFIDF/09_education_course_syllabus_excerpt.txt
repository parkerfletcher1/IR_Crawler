COURSE SYLLABUS EXCERPT

Course focus: foundational concepts, practical exercises, and weekly discussion.
Students will read assigned chapters, complete projects, and submit reflections.
Participation matters; attendance and engagement are part of the grade.

Assessment: quizzes, a midterm, and a final project. The final project includes
a proposal, a draft, peer feedback, and a presentation. Late work policies are
outlined in the course calendar.

Keywords: syllabus, curriculum, learning outcomes, rubric, assignment, grading,
feedback, and academic integrity.
