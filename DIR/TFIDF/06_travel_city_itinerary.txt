TRAVEL ITINERARY (3 DAYS)

Day 1: Arrive, check in, and walk the historic district. Visit a museum, then
eat at a local café. Evening: riverfront path and sunset photos.

Day 2: Morning market for snacks and coffee. Afternoon: guided tour, then a
short hike to a scenic overlook. Evening: live music and a late dinner.

Day 3: Neighborhood exploration by public transit. Stop at a bookstore, a
small gallery, and a park. Before departure, review the budget, receipts, and
souvenirs.

Travel keywords: itinerary, transit, lodging, reservations, budget, walking,
maps, and time zones.
