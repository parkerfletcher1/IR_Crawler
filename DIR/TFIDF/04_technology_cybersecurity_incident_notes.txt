CYBERSECURITY INCIDENT NOTES

At 09:14 an employee reported suspicious email activity. The message looked like
a routine invoice but contained a shortened link. Multiple recipients clicked
the link and entered credentials. Indicators suggest a phishing campaign with
credential harvesting.

Actions taken: reset passwords, revoke sessions, and enable multi-factor
authentication. Network logs were reviewed for unusual outbound connections.
Endpoint scans found no ransomware, but browser tokens may have been exposed.

Keywords for analysis: phishing, credentials, MFA, session revocation, logs,
IOC (indicator of compromise), containment, remediation, and user awareness
training.
