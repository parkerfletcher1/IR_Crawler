CLIMATE BRIEFING

Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide and methane trap heat in the atmosphere.
Rising concentrations contribute to warming trends, changing precipitation,
and more frequent extremes in some regions. Mitigation includes reducing
emissions, improving efficiency, and expanding renewable energy.

Adaptation includes resilient infrastructure, water planning, and community
preparedness for heat waves and floods. Measuring progress requires data,
monitoring, and transparent reporting.

Keywords: climate, carbon, emissions, mitigation, adaptation, renewable energy,
resilience, and monitoring.
